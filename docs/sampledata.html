Sample Data

The data below is a sample of our full dataset used in order to train our machine learning model. The full dataset contains more than 70,000 rows of patient data.

Here is a brief explanation of each feature of our sample data:
  • Age: age in days
  • Gender: 1 for woman and 2 for man
  • Height: in centimeters
  • Weight: in kilograms
  • Ap_hi: systolic blood pressure
  • Ap_low: distolic blood pressure
  • Cholesterol: 1-normal, 2-above normal, 3-well above normal
  • Gluc: glucose level, 1-normal, 2-above normal, 3-well above normal
  • Smoke: whether the patient smokes, 0-no, 1-yes
  • Alco: whether the patient drinks alcohol, 0-no, 1-yes
  • Active: whether the patient regularly does physical activities, 0-no, 1-yes
  • Cardio: presence or absense of cardiovascular disease, 0-no, 1-yes, this is our target variable
  • age_to_years: age in years
  • age_group: patient's age by age bucket
